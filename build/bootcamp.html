About Me

Charles Alade is the Chief Operating officer of Hebbymix studios limited and currently the General manager of Royal Roots 92.9FM Ibadan , He is an international voice artist , a seasoned broadcaster, Talent manager and conference speaker . As a media executive his body of work experience spans across numerous stations including Premier FM, kings Fm, Star fM and Solutions Fm where he hosted diverse shows ranging from culture, commerce, entertainment to Godly living. as a musician and multi-instrumentalist,he has performed and recorded numerous gospel hits including “Alapanla”, “Caesar”, “Special To Me”. With numerous awards including “Nigeria broadcasters merit award team song winner 2018 – Phenom Entertainment Awards 2021”, His voice covers a wide range of outputs, commercials, audio books , coperate videos, promos for many leading organizations across the globe, He is the convener of The voice over bootcamp a platform where Profesional voice artiste are trained and exported globally .

He is the founder of the Charles Alade inititiave ., A charity organsition whose vision is to improve the standard of living of everyone that is disadvantaged in the society . An author of many books , he is not slowing down soon. Besides managing his various studio concerns, Charles Alade serves in the capacity of Senior Pastor with oversight of the The Exchange Church Samonda, Ibadan. His early Sunday morning show Radio Prophectic prayers is a testament of God’s grace over his life and ministry. He is happily married to Afolake and they are blessed with two amazing children , Ireayomide and Momoreoluwa .
