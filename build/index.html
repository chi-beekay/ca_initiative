About Us

What We Do!!

THE CHARLES ALADE INITIATIVE, in a bid to address this challenge of the boy child’s education and development in Oyo State, Nigeria is on a mission to fill a void by combating the unintended perception of neglect of the boy child’s needs and aim to reduce the cost of education with her ‘project 1000’ mission, by providing 1000 new school uniforms to the boy child in government secondary schools across the 10 geographical zones within the state.

Our Vision Donating free 1000 new school uniforms to the Boy Child in Oyo State Government Schools by providing 100 uniforms to 2 government schools in a month.

Our mission

  • Giving the boy child the reason to love going to school .
